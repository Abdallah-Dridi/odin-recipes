Slow-Cooker Pepper Steak Recipe

Description

Slow-Cooker Pepper Steak: Comfort Food, Perfected

Craving a hearty and flavorful meal that practically cooks itself? Look no further than our Slow-Cooker Pepper Steak! Tender beef, vibrant bell peppers, and onions simmer together in a rich, savory sauce for a truly unforgettable taste.

This hands-off recipe is perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends. Serve over rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingrediants

  • Beef sirloin, cut into strips
  • Green bell peppers, chopped
  • Onion, chopped
  • Beef bouillon cube
  • Hot water
  • Cornstarch
  • Stewed tomatoes
  • Soy sauce
  • White sugar
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Vegetable oil (optional, for browning beef)

Steps

  1. If desired, brown the beef strips in a skillet with a bit of oil until golden brown.
  2. Place the beef strips in the slow cooker.
  3. Add chopped onions and green bell peppers to the slow cooker.
  4. Dissolve beef bouillon cube in hot water.
  5. Combine cornstarch with a small amount of cold water to create a slurry.
  6. Pour stewed tomatoes, soy sauce, sugar, and salt into the slow cooker.
  7. Stir in the dissolved bouillon and cornstarch slurry.
  8. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  9. Optional: Add garlic powder to taste before serving.
