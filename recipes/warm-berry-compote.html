Warm Berry Compote Recipe

Description

Luscious Berry Bliss: Warm Compote

A burst of summer sunshine in every spoonful. Warm Berry Compote is a delightful blend of fresh or frozen berries simmered in a sweet, tangy sauce. Perfect for topping pancakes, waffles, or ice cream, this versatile dessert topping is also delicious over yogurt or as a fruit-forward filling for tarts and pies.

Enjoy the luscious combination of juicy berries and aromatic spices in this simple yet irresistible treat.

Ingrediants

  • Fresh berries (such as strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, or blackberries)
  • Sugar
  • Lemon juice
  • Cornstarch
  • Water
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine berries, sugar, and lemon juice.
  2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water until smooth.
  4. Slowly whisk the cornstarch slurry into the berry mixture.
  5. Continue to cook and stir until the sauce thickens, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract (if using).
  7. Serve warm or let cool completely.
